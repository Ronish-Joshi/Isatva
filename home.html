    KEY ACTIVES
    BELLIS PERRNNIS FLOWER

    Bellis Pernnis
    Flower Extract

    Bellis Pernnis is a natural extract derived from daisy flowers providing
    an even and luminous complexion. It’s used in products for its oils and
    ammoniacal salts content, and is used as a skin-conditioning agent in
    products. It is especially safe for sensitive skin types and is a natural
    nontoxic alternative for skin-brightening, as the extract influences and
    can block different pathways which form melanin, effectively
    preventing pigmentation and dark spots.
